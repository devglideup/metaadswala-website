On-Page vs Off-Page SEO Explained

Understanding the two pillars of SEO that our best & affordable SEO Marketing Service covers comprehensively.

On-Page SEO

Everything you control directly on your website to improve rankings.

  • Title Tags & Meta Descriptions: Optimized for click-through rates
  • Header Structure: H1-H6 hierarchy with keywords
  • Content Optimization: E-E-A-T focused writing
  • Internal Linking: Strategic link architecture
  • Image Optimization: Alt tags, compression, WebP
  • URL Structure: Clean, keyword-rich URLs
  • Schema Markup: Rich snippets for better CTR

Off-Page SEO

Activities outside your website that build authority and trust.

  • High-Authority Backlinks: DA 50+ quality links
  • Guest Posting: Niche-relevant publications
  • Digital PR: Brand mentions & press coverage
  • Local Citations: NAP consistency across directories
  • Social Signals: Social media presence & engagement
  • Review Management: Google reviews & reputation
  • HARO Outreach: Journalist query responses
